UJUDGE

Judging an event has never been so easy

It is time to change the way events are run

UJudge is a completely free online judging system that can be customized to meet your event needs.

With the ability to keep track and manage all your event details, UJudge will turn your backyard skateboard competition into the next X-Games.

Wanna see whats under the hood? Checkout the code on Github.

3,2,1 Judge!

Create

Setting up an event could never be easier. After setting up your time limit and rounds, just add players and judges and you are good to go!

Admin

Next you are ready to run your event! Just select the event you created and start the timer. Now you can sit back and watch the players as the judges do their job.

Judge

Judging an event can be a lot of hard work, but not with UJudge! Just sign in from anywhere in the world and submit your score with the click of a button!

Looking to run an event without judges?

Don’t worry. We have you covered if your event doesn’t require judges. If you are looking to put 2 teams head to head and all you need is a quick scoreboard, we have you covered.

Best part is you don’t even need to make an account, so why not give it a try.
